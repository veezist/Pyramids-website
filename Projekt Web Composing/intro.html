Introduction


What are pyramids?

They are monumental building in the shape of a proper pyramid with a triangular, quadrangular or any polygonal shape. However, pyramids with a quadrangular base are the most popular ones. The pyramids are built in such a way that most of the weight is closer to the ground, thanks to which less material pushing down from above. In this way, the buildings are stable.


Where are pyramids?

The most famous pyramid complexes are found in Egypt and pre-Columbian America but it can be found in Greece and Mesopotamia as well.
